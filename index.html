About TechRitz

TechRitz is the annual international tech fest hosted by Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology, celebrating innovation, creativity, and technological excellence. With 40+ events spanning coding, robotics, AI, design, e-sports, and more, TechRitz provides a platform for students to showcase their skills, explore new technologies, and compete for exciting prizes.

The fest brings together students from RIT and other colleges, fostering collaboration, learning, and healthy competition. Participants can take part in technical and non-technical events, including hackathons, debates, technical shows, and creative challenges.

At TechRitz, we believe in the possibilities—encouraging young innovators to think big, experiment fearlessly, and push the boundaries of technology. With an overall institution trophy, cash prizes, and recognition for talent, TechRitz promises an unforgettable experience for every tech enthusiast.
